Extra classes will be ordered by class ID, and then by Servant ID. Once you've picked your target, there will be links below them to Atlas Academy Database so you can quickly check their kits and features. REMEMBER: All displayed banner information is strictly speculation until confirmed via official announcements. Site performance might be affected a bit depending on your network conditions, since data needs to be live-fetched from both Atlas Academy and the Banners by Servant Google Spreadsheet.
